Recent projects

AP Invoice Processing

Lead Developer of a lighthouse project that combines SAP Fiori and SAP Screen Personas in parallel in order to increase the efficiency of invoice processing processes at the largest food company in the world. Additionally, for analytical purposes SAP Smart Business applications with custom SAP HANA views were developed.

Fiori Launchpad XXL

Lead Developer in one of the largest SAP Fiori Launchpad rollouts worldwide. The Launchpad is designed to be the central entry point for employees for any HR related topic. Various custom implementations were necessary, e.g. an improved personalization concept, a fine granular authorization concept and the integration of apps residing on different servers. The total number of users is over 100.000.

My Approvals

Lead Developer and Architect of an extensive framework for a custom My Inbox Fiori application that is designed to be a one stop shop for approvals in one of the largest pharma companies of the world. Part of the project was also a custom UI5 template framework that supports different views for different types of approvals. Additional proof of concepts were implemented to have push notifications and offline capability.

Hybrid Requisition Approvals

Lead Developer and Coach of a packaged Approve Requisitions Fiori application at the largest telecommunication company of the Philippines. The application is wrapped in a SAP Fiori Client and targets iOS and Android devices. Custom adaptions of the SAP Fiori Client were necessary to support custom SAML authentication behaviour and to have an offline mode while using the application in remote locations.

KPI Dashboard on SAP CP

Lead Developer of an high performant KPI dashboard application for board members of one of the biggest insurance companies in the world. This Fiori application makes use of OData services exposed via HANA XS on SAP Cloud Platform.

CRM for Football

Lead Developer and Coach of the SAP CRM on HANA implementation at Germany's largest football association. The focus was on Activity and Campaign Management to efficiently manage event invitations and make this as smooth as possible for VIPs.
